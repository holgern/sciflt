member_functions

Member functions implemented in sciFLT toolbox.

Description

sciFLT toolbox implements the followings member functions :

  • Triangular

  • Trapezoidal

  • Gaussian

  • Extended Gaussian

  • Generalized Bell

  • Sigmoidal

  • Product of two Sigmoidal

  • Absolute difference of tho Sigmoidal

  • S-Shaped

  • Z-Shaped

  • Pi-Shaped

  • Constant (as output for Takagi-Sugeno)

  • Linear (as output for Takagi-Sugeno)
