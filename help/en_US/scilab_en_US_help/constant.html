constant

Constant output for Takagi-Sugeno fuzzy logic systems.

Calling Sequence

y=constant(x,par)

Parameters

x:

matrix of real with size [m,n].

par:

scalar .

Description

constant compute the constant output of a Takagi-Sugeno fls due the evaluations of rules stored in x. This is a util function.
