repvec

Repeat vector

Calling Sequence

y=repvec(m,x)

Parameters

x:

matrix of real.

m:

integer

Description

repvec repeat m times the vector x in the form y(1:m,:)=x.

Examples

// Repeat 10 times a vector
repvec(10,[1 2 3 4])
